Privacy
Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and accessible only to you through your account. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to render typefaces in the interface. No personal or financial data is shared with Google or any other third-party service. We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
